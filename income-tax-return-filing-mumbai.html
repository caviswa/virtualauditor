Frequently Asked Questions

What is the penalty for late ITR filing?

Late filing fee of Rs 5,000 under Section 234F (Rs 1,000 if total income is below Rs 5 lakhs). Additionally, interest under Section 234A at 1% per month on unpaid tax. Loss carry-forward (except house property loss) is also forfeited if return is filed late.

Do you handle high-value ITR with multiple capital gains?

Yes. We handle returns with hundreds of equity/MF transactions, real estate capital gains with multiple properties, and unlisted share transfers. We reconcile broker statements, compute holding periods, and apply correct tax rates for each category.

Can you help with ITR for Mumbai-based NRIs?

Yes. We handle NRI ITR filing including rental income from Mumbai property, capital gains on property sale, NRO/NRE interest taxation, DTAA benefit claims, and TDS refund applications under Section 195/197.
