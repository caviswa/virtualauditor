GST Registration Cancellation & Revocation

GST cancellation may be voluntary (business closure, turnover below threshold) or suo moto by the officer (non-filing, dormancy, fraud). Voluntary cancellation requires Form GST REG-16 and final return GSTR-10 within 3 months. Suo moto cancellation can be challenged through revocation application (Form GST REG-21) within 30 days. People Also Ask

What is the GST registration threshold in India?

Mandatory for turnover above ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services). ₹10 lakhs for special category states. Compulsory regardless of turnover for e-commerce sellers and interstate suppliers.

What is the penalty for late GST return filing?

Late fee of ₹50/day (₹20 for nil returns) under Section 47, capped at ₹5,000 per return period. Interest at 18% p.a. on tax liability under Section 50.

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I cancel GST registration voluntarily?

When business is discontinued, transferred, or turnover falls below threshold. File REG-16 and GSTR-10 (final return) within 3 months of cancellation.

My GST was cancelled suo moto. Can I revoke?

Yes. File Form GST REG-21 within 30 days of cancellation order. Clear all pending returns and pay outstanding tax before applying. We handle the revocation process including return backlog clearance.

How to cancel GST registration?

File GST REG-16 on the GST portal. Reason for cancellation required. Clear all pending returns and liabilities. Pay final tax. Officer verifies and issues cancellation order in REG-19. Effective from date specified in order.

Can cancelled GST registration be revoked?

Yes. File application for revocation in REG-21 within 30 days of cancellation order (if cancelled by officer). Suo motu cancelled registrations can be revoked if all pending returns and taxes are filed and paid.

Is final return (GSTR-10) mandatory after cancellation?

Yes. File GSTR-10 within 3 months of cancellation date or date of cancellation order, whichever is later. Contains details of stock held, ITC reversed, and tax payable. Penalty for non-filing: ₹200/day.

What are grounds for compulsory cancellation by officer?

Non-filing of returns for 6 consecutive months (regular) or 3 consecutive quarters (QRMP). Business discontinued. Obtained registration by fraud. Non-commencement of business within 6 months of voluntary registration.

What happens to input tax credit on stock after cancellation?

ITC on inputs held in stock and capital goods must be reversed in GSTR-10 as per Section 29(5). Pay tax on stock at applicable rate or ITC amount, whichever is higher.
