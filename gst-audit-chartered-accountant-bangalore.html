Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs GST audit in Bangalore?

GST audit (GSTR-9C) is required for taxpayers with aggregate turnover exceeding Rs 5 crores in a financial year. The reconciliation statement must be certified by a qualified Chartered Accountant.

What is the deadline for GSTR-9C filing?

GSTR-9C is filed along with GSTR-9 (annual return) by 31 December of the following financial year. For FY 2024-25, the due date is 31 December 2025.

What happens if GST audit reveals additional tax liability?

Any additional tax liability identified during GST audit must be paid through DRC-03 (voluntary payment). Late payment attracts interest at 18% per annum. Proactive disclosure and payment can avoid penalties under Section 73/74.
