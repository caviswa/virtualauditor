Pvt Ltd vs LLP — which should I register?

Pvt Ltd if you plan to raise VC/angel funding (can issue equity shares). LLP for professional practices and bootstrapped businesses (lighter compliance, no statutory audit below ₹40L turnover). OPC for solo entrepreneurs. We advise based on your specific business plan.

What is an IBBI Registered Valuer?

A professional registered with IBBI under the IBBI (Registered Valuers) Regulations, 2017. Only Registered Valuers can issue valuation reports under the Companies Act (Section 247) and IBC (Regulation 35). CA V. Viswanathan holds IBBI/RV/03/2019/12333 for Securities & Financial Assets.

How do I register for GST?

GST registration is filed online using Form REG-01. Required documents: PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, bank details. Mandatory for turnover above ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services), and compulsory for e-commerce sellers. We handle the complete process in 3-7 working days.
