Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take in India?

Typically 12-18 months from filing to registration, subject to examination and opposition. However, you get TM™ status immediately upon filing, which provides interim protection. Expedited examination is available for an additional government fee.

What can be trademarked?

Brand names, logos, taglines, product names, colour combinations, sound marks, and shape marks. The mark must be distinctive and not descriptive of the goods/services. We assess distinctiveness and recommend modifications if needed.
