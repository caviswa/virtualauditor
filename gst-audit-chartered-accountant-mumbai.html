Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnover limit for mandatory GST audit?

GST audit (GSTR-9C reconciliation statement) is mandatory for taxpayers with aggregate turnover exceeding Rs 5 crores in a financial year. This applies per GSTIN — multi-state businesses must check each registration separately.

Can the same CA do both statutory audit and GST audit?

Yes. There is no restriction under GST law against the statutory auditor also certifying GSTR-9C. However, some companies prefer independent GST audit for additional assurance. Virtual Auditor can serve in either capacity.

What documents are needed for GST audit?

Audited financial statements, trial balance with GST-wise breakup, all GST returns (GSTR-1, 3B, 2A/2B), e-way bill records, credit/debit notes, and RCM payment details. We provide a comprehensive checklist at engagement start.
