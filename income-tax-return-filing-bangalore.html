Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle RSU/ESOP taxation for Bangalore IT employees?

We compute perquisite value at exercise (difference between FMV and exercise price), claim employer TDS credit, and compute capital gains at sale. For US-listed company RSUs, we also claim DTAA credit for taxes paid in the US through foreign tax credit (Form 67).

Can startups claim tax holiday through ITR filing?

Yes. DPIIT-recognised startups can claim a 3-year tax holiday under Section 80-IAC. We handle the eligibility verification, Inter-Ministerial Board certification process, and correct ITR schedule filing to claim this benefit.

What documents do I need for ITR filing?

Form 16 (salaried), AIS/TIS from income tax portal, bank statements, capital gains statements from brokers, investment proofs (80C, 80D, HRA), and home loan certificate. We provide a detailed checklist at engagement start.
