Frequently Asked Questions

What is the penalty for late ROC filing in Bangalore?

Late filing attracts additional fees of Rs 100 per day per form with no upper cap. For AOC-4 and MGT-7 combined, the penalty can exceed Rs 73,000 per year. Directors may also face personal liability for non-compliance.

What forms are required for annual ROC compliance?

Mandatory annual forms: AOC-4 (financial statements, due within 30 days of AGM), MGT-7/MGT-7A (annual return, due within 60 days of AGM), and DIR-3 KYC (director KYC, due 30 September annually). Additional event-based forms may apply.

Can you handle overdue ROC filings from previous years?

Yes. We handle compliance catch-up including filing of all overdue AOC-4 and MGT-7 forms, payment of additional fees, and condonation of delay applications with MCA where applicable.
