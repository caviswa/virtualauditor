Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Virtual CFO and how is it different from an accountant?

A Virtual CFO provides strategic financial leadership — fundraising, cash flow management, financial planning, investor reporting — while an accountant handles bookkeeping and compliance. Virtual CFO is forward-looking (planning and strategy), while accounting is backward-looking (recording transactions).
