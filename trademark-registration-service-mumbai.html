Frequently Asked Questions

Can I file a trademark application for multiple classes in Mumbai?

Yes. India allows multi-class applications under one filing. Each additional class requires a separate government fee (Rs 4,500/9,000 per class). We advise on which classes are essential for your business and brand protection strategy.

What happens if my trademark application receives an objection?

Objections are common and not a rejection. We file a detailed reply (TM-O) with legal arguments, evidence of use, and distinctiveness justification. If the reply is accepted, the mark proceeds to publication. If not, a hearing is scheduled.

How do I protect my trademark internationally from Mumbai?

India is a member of the Madrid Protocol. You can file an international application through the Indian Trademark Registry designating any member country. We handle the complete Madrid Protocol filing process.
