Frequently Asked Questions

What is the due date for income tax return filing?

31 July for individuals and non-audit cases. 31 October for businesses requiring audit. 30 November for transfer pricing cases. Belated returns can be filed until 31 December of the assessment year with a late fee of Rs 5,000.

Should I choose the old or new tax regime?

The choice depends on your deductions and exemptions. If your total deductions (80C, 80D, HRA, home loan) exceed Rs 3-4 lakhs, the old regime may be beneficial. We compute tax under both regimes and recommend the optimal choice.

Do you handle NRI income tax returns from Chennai?

Yes. We file NRI returns with DTAA benefit claims, foreign tax credit computation, NRO/NRE income treatment, and capital gains on Indian property sales. We also handle Section 195 TDS certificates for NRI property transactions.
