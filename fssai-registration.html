What is FSSAI registration? When Is FSSAI Registration (Food License) Not Required?

FSSAI registration may not be required when: (a) you are a farmer selling raw, unprocessed agricultural produce directly, (b) the food business is a petty manufacturer or street vendor whose annual turnover does not exceed Rs 12 lakhs (basic registration still required at no cost), (c) the business does not involve manufacturing, processing, packaging, or selling food products, or (d) the food activity is exempt under the FSSA 2006 (e.g., certain religious institutions distributing prasad). All other food businesses — restaurants, cloud kitchens, packaged food, catering — require FSSAI registration or licence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which FSSAI licence do I need?

Basic registration: turnover up to ₹12 lakhs. State licence: ₹12 lakhs to ₹20 crore. Central licence: above ₹20 crore, or if operating in 2+ states, or importers/exporters.

How long does FSSAI registration take?

Basic: 7 days. State: 30-60 days. Central: 60-90 days. Timelines depend on completeness of documentation and state food authority processing.

When is FSSAI registration mandatory?

Mandatory for any food business operator — manufacturers, processors, distributors, transporters, retailers, importers, storage, and e-commerce food sellers. Turnover-based: Basic Registration (<₹12 lakhs), State Licence (₹12-20 crore), Central Licence (>₹20 crore).

What documents are needed for FSSAI registration?

Photo ID, address proof, food safety management plan, list of food products, layout plan of premises, NOC from municipality, source of raw material, and water test report. Additional documents for manufacturers: production capacity details.

What is the penalty for operating without FSSAI licence?

Fine up to ₹5 lakhs under Section 63 of Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. Imprisonment possible for serious violations. Product recall and business closure orders possible.

Is FSSAI needed for cloud kitchen or home-based food business?

Yes. All food businesses need at minimum FSSAI Basic Registration, including home-based businesses, cloud kitchens, and food delivery through Zomato/Swiggy. Basic Registration costs ₹100 for 1 year.
