Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed for fdi share pricing under fema?

Key documents include: Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, share subscription/transfer agreement, valuation report, board resolution, FIRC from the bank, and details of the foreign investor. We provide a personalised checklist after the initial consultation.

How long does fdi share pricing under fema take?

Standard filings: 7-10 working days. Compounding applications: 15-21 working days for preparation (RBI processing takes additional 60-90 days). Contact us at +91-99622 60333 for your specific timeline.

What happens if FEMA compliance is not done on time?

FEMA contraventions attract penalty up to 3x the transaction amount under Section 13, plus Rs 5,000/day for continuing contravention. Compounding with RBI is possible but becomes more expensive with delay. Early compliance is always advisable.
