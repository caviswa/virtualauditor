Frequently Asked Questions

Is IBBI registration mandatory for business valuation?

IBBI registration is mandatory for valuations under Companies Act Section 247 (share allotment, buyback, merger schemes) and IBC (CIRP, liquidation). For income tax and FEMA valuations, a CA or merchant banker can provide valuation, but IBBI-registered valuer reports carry higher credibility.

How much does a business valuation cost in Mumbai?

Fees depend on company size, complexity, purpose, and number of methods required. We provide a fixed-fee quote after understanding the engagement scope. Contact +91-77000 89597 for a no-obligation discussion.

Can you value a company for FEMA share pricing?

Yes. We regularly provide FEMA-compliant valuations for share issuance to foreign investors (FC-GPR), share transfers between residents and non-residents (FC-TRS), and exit pricing under FEMA regulations. Our valuations comply with RBI pricing guidelines.
