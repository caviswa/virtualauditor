Our Process

Step 1: Free consultation to understand your business structure, shareholder pattern, and capital requirements. Step 2: DSC procurement for all directors and DIN application (if not already held). Step 3: Name reservation through RUN (Reserve Unique Name) or Part B of SPICe+. Step 4: Drafting of MOA and AOA customised to your business objects and shareholder agreement terms. Step 5: SPICe+ (INC-32) filing with MCA for incorporation, PAN, TAN, and GST registration simultaneously. Step 6: Certificate of Incorporation received — typically within 5-7 working days of filing. Step 7: Post-incorporation setup: statutory registers, first board resolution, share certificate issuance, and compliance calendar.
