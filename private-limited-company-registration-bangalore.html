Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Pvt Ltd company registration take in Bangalore?

5-7 working days from SPICe+ filing. Including name reservation and DSC, the total timeline is 7-10 working days. We provide a clear timeline at the start of the engagement.

What is the minimum capital required for a Pvt Ltd company in Bangalore?

There is no minimum paid-up capital requirement under the Companies Act, 2013. However, the authorised capital determines the ROC filing fee (Rs 500 for up to Rs 1 lakh authorised capital). We recommend Rs 1-10 lakh authorised capital for startups.

Can Virtual Auditor help with DPIIT Startup India registration after incorporation?

Yes. We file DPIIT Startup India recognition immediately after incorporation, which unlocks tax benefits under Section 80-IAC (3 years of tax holiday) and angel tax exemption under Section 56(2)(viib). The DPIIT application is typically approved within 48 hours.
