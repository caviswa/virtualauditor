When Is Nidhi Company Registration Not Required?

Nidhi Company registration may not be required when: (a) you cannot meet the minimum 200-member requirement within one year of incorporation, (b) the Net Owned Funds will not reach Rs 10 lakhs as required, (c) the ratio of net owned funds to deposits is expected to exceed 1:20 limit, (d) you prefer an NBFC structure for broader financial activities, or (e) a cooperative society structure better suits your membership and geography. Nidhi Companies can only accept deposits from and lend to their members — no public deposits or lending.

Who Needs Nidhi Company Registration?

This registration is required for: (a) businesses seeking limited liability protection for promoters and directors, (b) startups planning to raise equity funding from investors (angel/VC/PE), (c) entities requiring a separate legal identity for contracts, property, and bank accounts, (d) businesses planning to scale operations across multiple states, (e) professionals or consultants seeking to formalise their practice into a body corporate, and (f) any person or group mandated by law to register under the applicable business structure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Nidhi Company accept deposits from non-members?

No. Nidhi Companies can only accept deposits from and lend to their own members. They cannot deal with non-members.

What is the minimum membership?

Minimum 200 members within 1 year of incorporation. Net owned funds: ₹10 lakhs. Ratio of NOF to deposits: 1:20.

What is a Nidhi company?

Mutual benefit society under Section 406 of Companies Act. Members save and borrow from the company. Cannot deal with non-members. Governed by Nidhi Rules 2014. Similar to cooperative bank but registered as company.

What are the requirements for Nidhi company?

Minimum 200 members within 1 year, net owned funds ₹20 lakhs, 1:20 NOF to deposit ratio, unencumbered term deposits minimum 10% of outstanding deposits. 3 directors required.

Can Nidhi company accept deposits from non-members?

No. Nidhi can only accept deposits from and lend to its members. Cannot issue debentures, cannot acquire another company, and cannot deal in any securities.

What is the lending limit for Nidhi company?

Maximum loan: ₹15 lakhs per member. Maximum against fixed deposit: 100% of deposit. Against other specified securities: limits apply. Interest rate: maximum 7.5% above highest rate offered on deposits.

Is FDI allowed in Nidhi company?

No. Nidhi companies are in the negative list for FDI. No foreign investment permitted. Only Indian residents can be members and shareholders.
