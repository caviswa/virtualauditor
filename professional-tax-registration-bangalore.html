Professional Tax Rates in Karnataka (2024-25)

Karnataka levies professional tax under the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976. The tax applies to every person engaged in any profession, trade, calling or employment in the state.

Employer obligation: Every employer in Karnataka must obtain PT Registration Certificate (PTRC) and deduct PT from employee salaries monthly. The employer must also pay ₹2,500 per year for the business establishment itself (PTEC). Returns must be filed monthly by the 20th of the following month.

Penalty for non-compliance: Interest at 1.25% per month on delayed payment. Penalty up to ₹1,000 per month for non-registration. The Commercial Tax Department can recover arrears as land revenue.

When Is Professional Tax Registration Not Required?

Professional Tax registration may not be required when: (a) all employees/professionals earn below the Karnataka PT threshold (currently Rs 15,000/month), (b) the business has no employees (sole proprietor with no staff), (c) the entity is exempt under the Karnataka Tax on Professions Act, or (d) PT is already being deducted by the employer. Employers with salaried employees in Karnataka must register for PT deduction.

Who Needs Professional Tax Registration?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PT rate in Karnataka?

Employees: ₹200/month (salary >₹15,000). Self-employed: ₹2,500/year. Rates specified in Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976.

What is professional tax in Karnataka?

Direct tax on persons in profession, trade, or employment. Maximum Rs 2,500/year. Employer registration (PTRC) mandatory.

What are PT slab rates in Karnataka?

Up to Rs 15,000/month: Nil. Rs 15,001-25,000: Rs 200/month. Above Rs 25,000: Rs 200/month (max Rs 2,500/year).

What is the due date for PT?

Monthly by 20th of following month. Late payment: 1.25% interest/month. Non-registration penalty: Rs 1,000/month.

Is PT deductible from income tax?

Yes. Deductible under Section 16(iii) of Income Tax Act from gross salary income. Employer claims as business expenditure.

Does PT apply to company directors?

Directors drawing salary within PT slab are covered. Non-executive directors with only sitting fees are generally not covered.
