Frequently Asked Questions

How long does LLP registration take in Mumbai?

5-8 working days from FiLLiP filing. Total timeline including DPIN, DSC, and name reservation: 8-12 working days. Contact us at +91-77000 89597 for exact timeline.

What is the difference between LLP and Private Limited Company?

LLP offers limited liability with lower compliance (no statutory audit if turnover < Rs 40 lakhs, no board meetings). However, LLPs cannot raise equity funding from VCs/PEs. Choose Pvt Ltd if you plan to raise investment; choose LLP for professional practices and businesses not seeking equity capital.

Can an existing partnership convert to LLP in Mumbai?

Yes. Chapter X of the LLP Act allows conversion of partnership firms to LLP without dissolution. All assets, liabilities, and contracts transfer automatically. We handle the complete conversion process.
