Frequently Asked Questions

What is the due date for ROC annual filing?

AOC-4 is due within 30 days of AGM. MGT-7 is due within 60 days of AGM. AGM must be held within 6 months of financial year end (i.e., by 30 September for March year-end companies). DIR-3 KYC is due annually by 30 September.
