IBBI Registered Valuers are the only professionals authorised to issue valuation reports required under the Companies Act (Section 247) and the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (CIRP Regulation 35). The registration requires: (a) membership of a Recognised Valuer Organisation (RVO), (b) completion of the IBBI valuation examination, (c) minimum 3 years of relevant experience, and (d) meeting fit and proper criteria.
