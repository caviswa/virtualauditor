What happens if ROC annual filing is late?

Penalty of ₹100/day per form (AOC-4 and MGT-7 separately) with no maximum cap. Persistent non-filing can lead to company strike-off and director disqualification under Section 164(2).

Is statutory audit mandatory for all companies?

Yes for all companies registered under the Companies Act, 2013. For LLPs: only if turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs or contribution exceeds ₹25 lakhs.

When Is LLP Annual Filing Not Required?

LLP annual filing exemption is not available for active LLPs — every LLP must file Form 8 and Form 11 annually regardless of revenue or activity level. The only exception is when the LLP has applied for strike-off and the application is pending. Non-filing attracts Rs 100/day penalty per form (no maximum cap). LLPs that have not filed for 2 consecutive years risk being flagged for striking off by ROC.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is Form 8 due?

Within 30 days from end of 6 months of FY = October 30 for March year-end LLPs.

When is Form 11 due?

Within 60 days from FY end = May 30 for March year-end LLPs.

Is LLP audit needed for annual filing?

Only if turnover >₹40 lakhs OR contribution >₹25 lakhs. Below both: file Form 8 based on books maintained by partners.

What are the annual filings for an LLP?

Form 8 (Statement of Account and Solvency): within 30 days of 6 months from year-end (by October 30 for March year-end). Form 11 (Annual Return): within 60 days of year-end (by May 30). Income Tax Return: by July 31 or October 31.

What is the penalty for late LLP filing?

Form 8 and Form 11: ₹100 per day per form. No cap. Example: 6 months delay = ₹180 × ₹100 × 2 = ₹36,000. After 2 years of non-filing: RoC can initiate strike-off proceedings.

Is audit required for LLP annual filing?

Only if turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs OR partner contribution exceeds ₹25 lakhs. Below thresholds: prepare accounts and file statements without audit. This is LLP major compliance advantage.

Can LLP annual filing be done without CA?

Form 8 and Form 11 can be self-filed. But Statement of Account requires proper accounting. If audit is required: CA must sign. We recommend professional preparation to avoid errors that attract RoC scrutiny.

What happens if LLP does not file for 2 years?

RoC initiates strike-off proceedings. LLP name appears on strike-off list. Partners remain liable for debts and penalties even after strike-off. DINs/DPINs may face restrictions. File all pending returns immediately.
