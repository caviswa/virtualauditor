Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications does your valuer hold?

CA V. Viswanathan is an IBBI Registered Valuer (Securities or Financial Assets, Reg. No. IBBI/RV/03/2019/12333), Fellow Chartered Accountant (FCA), and Associate Company Secretary (ACS). IBBI registration is mandatory for valuations under Companies Act Section 247 and IBC.

How long does a business valuation take?

Typically 7-15 working days depending on complexity, data availability, and purpose. Urgent valuations for regulatory deadlines can be delivered in 3-5 days with premium pricing.

What valuation methods do you use?

We apply multiple methods — DCF (income approach), comparable company multiples (market approach), and NAV (asset approach) — and triangulate to arrive at fair value. The method weightage depends on the business type, stage, and valuation purpose.
