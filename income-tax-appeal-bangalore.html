Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time limit for filing income tax appeal?

Appeal to CIT(A): 30 days from date of assessment order. Appeal to ITAT: 60 days from CIT(A) order. Condonation of delay is possible with valid reasons but not guaranteed — timely filing is critical.

Can I get a stay on income tax demand during appeal?

Yes. You can apply for stay of demand under Section 220(6) to the Assessing Officer, or file a stay petition with CIT(A)/ITAT. Generally, payment of 20% of disputed demand is expected for granting stay (CBDT Circular).
