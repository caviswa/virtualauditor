Frequently Asked Questions

How long does LLP registration take in Bangalore?

5-8 working days from FiLLiP filing. Total timeline including DPIN and name reservation: 8-12 working days. We provide a clear project timeline at the start.

Is audit mandatory for an LLP in Bangalore?

No. LLP audit is mandatory only if turnover exceeds Rs 40 lakhs or partner contribution exceeds Rs 25 lakhs. Below these thresholds, only annual return (Form 11) and statement of accounts (Form 8) are required.
