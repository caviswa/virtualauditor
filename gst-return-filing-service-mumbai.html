What happens if GST returns are filed late?

Late filing attracts a fee of Rs 50/day (Rs 20/day for nil returns) capped at Rs 5,000 per return under Section 47. Additionally, interest at 18% per annum applies on outstanding tax under Section 50. Continuous non-filing for 6 months can lead to GST registration cancellation.
