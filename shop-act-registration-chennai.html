Frequently Asked Questions

Is shop act registration mandatory in Chennai?

Yes. All commercial establishments, shops, offices, and restaurants in Chennai must register within 30 days of commencing business under the Tamil Nadu Shops and Establishments Act, 1947. Non-registration attracts penalties under Section 30.

What documents are needed for shop act registration in Chennai?

PAN card, Aadhaar of proprietor/partners/directors, address proof of premises (rent agreement + NOC or ownership proof), nature of business details, and list of employees with joining dates.

How long does shop act registration take in Chennai?

Typically 7-15 working days from application submission. The Inspector of Labour may conduct a site inspection. We follow up proactively to ensure timely issuance.
